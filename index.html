Our Story

A few moments along the way

  1. 2019

    How we met

    A rainy afternoon in a Brooklyn bookstore. Henry reached for the same volume of Neruda that Amelia had just claimed.

  2. 2021

    Our first trip

    Two backpacks, one beat-up rental car, and a thousand miles up the Pacific coast. We knew then.

  3. 2024

    The proposal

    On the cliffs of Big Sur at sunset, with a single ring tucked inside a folded letter that Henry had been carrying for months.

  4. 2026

    Forever begins

    Surrounded by the people we love most, in a garden full of olive trees and lavender.
